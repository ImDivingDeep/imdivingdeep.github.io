Game developer

Project lead at Galaxy Technology

Like many others I delved into the world of Roblox as a kid. I started playing obstacle courses, RPGs, shooters and eventually found a knack in the build to survive genre where I was commended by many for my fortresses I built. This awakened in me the desire to create games. Around 2011 I was working on a little western RPG game on Roblox and when I got home from school one day I suddenly had thousands of people playing my game. I created a sequel and landed myself a spot as one of the top 300 developers of Roblox. I worked on many different projects after that and even though I haven't touched Roblox for a good couple years now, my username is still known amongst many.

After highschool I studied for Game Developer at Grafisch Lyceum Rotterdam, received my degree and after that I went to the Breda University of Applied Sciences where I studied International Game Architecture and Design for one year. I also worked for 'Real Life Gaming' in Breda. Personally still one of my favorite places to have worked. I got to walk around as a prison guard with 'Prison Escape' and coordinate a group of 8 to 10 people.

Now I am proudly working at Galaxy Technology as lead programmer and get to mess around with Unreal Engine for a living!
